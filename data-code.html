Variables Description

The dataset includes variables related to tuberculosis incidence along with:

  • Socioeconomic indicators (GDP, education, income)
  • Healthcare factors (hospital beds, access)
  • Environmental variables (temperature, precipitation)
  • Demographic variables (population, density)

Publication

Rahman, M.S. and Shiddik, A.B., 2025. Utilizing artificial intelligence to predict and analyze socioeconomic, environmental, and healthcare factors driving tuberculosis globally. Scientific Reports, 15(1), p.13619.
